SOON

What would you do if you wake up in the middle of the night in a remote gas station. It‘s raining heavily and you enter the building to seek help. But why there is no one around? Something doesn‘t seem right...
This is a game about adventure and puzzles that will make you question what‘s real and what‘s not. A psychedelic travel through one‘s mind.
